About Me

Architect. Builder. Problem Solver.

I'm Saundarya Pradhan, a Software Application Architect based in Pune, India, with over 12 years of hands-on experience designing and delivering production-grade software across fintech, NBFC, and enterprise domains.

My work sits at the intersection of backend architecture, frontend engineering, and emerging AI systems. I've led end-to-end builds, from low-level API design and database modelling to React-based UIs and cloud-native deployments on AWS, always with a focus on scale, security, and developer experience.

In the NBFC (Non-Banking Financial Company) space, I've architected critical financial workflows including payment orchestration, loan origination systems, and petabyte-scale document management, where correctness and reliability aren't optional, they're the product.

I thrive in roles where deep technical ownership meets business impact: mentoring engineering teams, defining architecture standards, and translating complex requirements into elegant, maintainable systems.
